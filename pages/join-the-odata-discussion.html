OData Discussion Group

We now use a new OData Discussion Group for discussion of OData related issues. You can ask or answer questions about the OData protocol either through email or directly through web page. We will still support old one for some time for you to move to the new one, then we will use this to replace the old ones.

Instructions

  • To subscribe to this group: send email to odata-discussion+subscribe@googlegroups.com
  • To unsubscribe to this group: send email to odata-discussion+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com
  • To post through email: send email to odata-discussion@googlegroups.com
  • To post and view topics through web: please visit OData Discussion Group

    Please note that although this is a Google group, you don't necessarily need a Google account to subscribe.

Notes

  • In this mailing list, you can post questions, feedbacks, bug reports and other issues related to OData.
  • Although we don't have strict rules of the content of your post, please be noted that advertisement and things not related to OData are not allowed to post to this mailing list. The admin for this mailing list can remove the inappropriate posts.
