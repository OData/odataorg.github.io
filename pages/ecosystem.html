Consumers

OData consumers are simply applications that consume data exposed using the OData protocol. OData consumers can vary greatly in sophistication, from something as simple as your web browser all the way through to a custom application that takes advantage of all the features of the OData Protocol. Simply pick a consumer from the list below and point it at one of the live services.
